Easy DIY Pumpkin Vases

To make a pumpkin vase all you will need is a pumpkin and some flowers from your grocery store!

  1. Start by cutting around the stem of your pumpkin, making a large enough opening to fit your flowers


  2. Empty the seeds from the pupkin and fill it with water


  3. Cut your flowers to desired length and fill up your new vase!
