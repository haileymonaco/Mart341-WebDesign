About Me

Hello! My name is Sydney Hanson and it is my goal to find you your perfect shelter dog. Whether you're needing an active dog to take with you on daily hikes, a couch potato to snuggle with, or a perfect family friendly dog, I am determined to find you the perfect match!
I have a major in zoology that I received at Carroll College in Helena, Montana. After training animal behavior for over 15 years, I have decided to focus on my passion: getting these dogs out of shelters and into loving homes.
